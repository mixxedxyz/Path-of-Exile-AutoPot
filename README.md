# AutoPod for Path of Exile 1 & 2

**AutoPod** is a lightweight external utility for **Path of Exile 1 and Path of Exile 2**, created to make flask usage faster, smoother, and more reliable during intense gameplay.

It works entirely through **pixel and color detection on your screen**.

. No injection  
. No memory editing  
. No game file modification  
. No invasive interaction with the game client  

AutoPod reacts only to what is visible on your monitor. Nothing more, nothing less.

---

## Why AutoPod Exists

At some point, I was simply tired of dying because I forgot to press my **health flask** in time.

Anyone who plays Path of Exile seriously knows how frustrating that feels.  
You survive dangerous mechanics, move correctly, position well, and still die because of one small missed flask input.

That is exactly why AutoPod was created.

It helps remove those avoidable mistakes by reacting faster and more consistently when your **Life** or **Mana** drops below the visible threshold on screen.

---

## What AutoPod Does

AutoPod monitors the areas you define on your screen, such as your **Life** and **Mana** sections.

When a selected color is no longer detected, AutoPod instantly sends the assigned key input.

### Default Example

. **1** = Health Flask  
. **2** = Mana Flask  

Example:

. If the mana color disappears, AutoPod presses your mana flask key automatically.  
. If life drops below the selected threshold, AutoPod reacts immediately.  

The result is simple:

. Faster reaction time  
. More reliable flask usage  
. Fewer unnecessary deaths  
. Smoother gameplay in stressful situations  

---

## What AutoPod Does Not Do

AutoPod can help prevent many avoidable deaths caused by delayed flask usage.

However, it does **not** protect you from **true one-hit mechanics**.

If a mechanic is designed to kill instantly, no flask automation can save you from that.  
A real one-shot is still a real one-shot.

AutoPod is built to reduce mistakes. It is not built to make you immortal.

## Setup Guide

### 1.1 Download AutoPod
Download the latest AutoPod release and extract the files if needed.

### 1.2 Start the Application
Launch **AutoPod** and wait until the interface is fully loaded.

### 1.3 Activate Your Trial or License
Enter your license key to activate AutoPod.

If you are a new user, you can start with the **free 24-hour trial license**.

---

### 2.1 Select the Game Area
Use the area selection tool to mark the visible game region correctly.

### 2.2 Select the Life Area
Mark the area where AutoPod should monitor your life flask trigger.

### 2.3 Select the Mana Area
Mark the area where AutoPod should monitor your mana flask trigger.

Make sure all areas are selected accurately for the best results.

---

### 3.1 Check Your Key Setup
Verify that your flask hotkeys match your in-game setup.

Example:

. **1** = Life Flask  
. **2** = Mana Flask  

### 3.2 Start AutoPod
Press **Start** in the application.

### 3.3 Play Normally
AutoPod will now monitor the selected screen areas and trigger the configured flask keys when needed.
